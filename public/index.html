💡 About DUDE

What is DUDE?

DUDE is your AI-powered assistant designed to help you with questions, creative tasks, coding, and more. Think of it as your smart companion that's always ready to chat!

How to Use

  • Start a Chat: Click "New Chat" or type in the input box
  • Ask Anything: Type your question and press Enter or click send
  • Upload Files: Click the + button to attach images or documents
  • Copy Responses: Click the copy button on any AI message
  • View History: Access past conversations from the history button

Credits System

Each message costs 5 credits. Watch a short ad to earn 20 credits instantly, or upgrade for unlimited access!

Tips for Best Results

  • Be specific and clear in your questions
  • Provide context when needed
  • Break complex tasks into smaller questions
  • Use the suggestions to get started
